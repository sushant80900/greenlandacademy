About Our School

25 Years of Educational Excellence

At Greenland Academy E.F. Sunsari P.V.T. L.T.D, we believe in providing quality education that prepares students for the challenges of tomorrow. Our comprehensive curriculum combines academic rigor with character development.

We foster a learning environment where students are encouraged to explore, question, and innovate. Our dedicated faculty ensures each child receives personalized attention to reach their full potential.

Educational Philosophy

At Greenland Academy, we believe that education is not just about imparting knowledge but about nurturing curiosity, fostering creativity, and building character. Our mission is to create a learning environment where every student feels valued, challenged, and inspired to reach their full potential.

With over 25 years of experience in educational leadership, I am committed to ensuring that our academy continues to be a beacon of excellence in education, preparing students not just for exams, but for life.
